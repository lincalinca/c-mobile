# AdMob Payment Setup - Quick Reference

## üè¶ Information Required for Payment Setup

When you click **"Add payment details"** in the AdMob dashboard, you'll need to provide:

### 1. Account Type Selection
- **Individual** (Personal account)
- **Business** (Company account)

### 2. Payment Method
Choose one of:
- **Bank Transfer** (Recommended for Australia)
- **Wire Transfer**
- **Other available options**

### 3. Bank Account Details (for Bank Transfer)

**Required Information:**
- **Account Holder Name:** Must exactly match your AdMob account name
- **Bank Name:** Full name of your bank
- **Account Number:** Your bank account number
- **BSB (Bank State Branch):** 6-digit code for Australian banks
  - Format: XXX-XXX (e.g., 062-000)
  - Find it on your bank statement or online banking
- **SWIFT/BIC Code:** Required for international transfers
  - Format: 8-11 characters (e.g., ANZBAU3M for ANZ Bank)
  - Your bank can provide this if needed
- **Bank Address:** Full address of your bank branch

### 4. Tax Information

**For Australian Accounts:**

**If Individual:**
- **Tax File Number (TFN):** Your personal TFN
- **Full Name:** As it appears on tax documents
- **Date of Birth:** Required for tax verification
- **Address:** Residential address

**If Business:**
- **ABN (Australian Business Number):** Your business ABN
- **Business Name:** Registered business name
- **Business Address:** Registered business address
- **Tax ID:** ABN serves as tax ID

### 5. Contact Information
- **Email:** Must match AdMob account email
- **Phone Number:** Contact number for verification
- **Address:** Physical address (must match tax documents)

### 6. Payment Currency
- Select **AUD (Australian Dollars)** as your payment currency

## üìã Step-by-Step Process

1. **Click "Add payment details"** on the Payments card
2. **Select Country:** Australia
3. **Choose Account Type:** Individual or Business
4. **Select Payment Method:** Bank Transfer
5. **Enter Bank Details:**
   - Account holder name
   - Bank name
   - Account number
   - BSB code
   - SWIFT code (if required)
   - Bank address
6. **Complete Tax Information:**
   - Enter TFN (individual) or ABN (business)
   - Verify name and address match tax records
7. **Review and Submit:**
   - Double-check all information
   - Submit for review
8. **Wait for Approval:**
   - Usually takes 24-48 hours
   - You'll receive email confirmation

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

### Account Name Matching
- The account holder name **must exactly match** your AdMob account name
- If there's a mismatch, AdMob may reject the payment setup
- Check your AdMob account settings to verify the exact name

### BSB Code Lookup
Common Australian bank BSB formats:
- **Commonwealth Bank:** 06XX-XXX
- **ANZ Bank:** 01XX-XXX
- **Westpac:** 03XX-XXX
- **NAB:** 08XX-XXX

You can find your BSB:
- On your bank statement
- In your online banking app
- On your bank card (sometimes)
- By calling your bank

### SWIFT Code Lookup
If you need your bank's SWIFT code:
- Check your bank's website
- Contact your bank directly
- Use online SWIFT code lookup tools
- Common Australian bank SWIFT codes:
  - **Commonwealth Bank:** CTBAAU2S
  - **ANZ Bank:** ANZBAU3M
  - **Westpac:** WPACAU2S
  - **NAB:** NATAAU3303M

### Tax Information Accuracy
- Ensure tax information matches your ATO records exactly
- Incorrect information can delay approval
- Business accounts require valid ABN

### Verification Process
- AdMob may verify your bank account with a small test deposit
- Check your bank account for a small verification amount
- You may need to confirm the amount in AdMob dashboard

## üö® Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: "Account name doesn't match"
**Solution:** 
- Verify exact spelling in AdMob account settings
- Use the exact name as it appears on your bank account
- Contact AdMob support if names are legitimately different

### Issue: "Invalid BSB code"
**Solution:**
- Double-check BSB format (XXX-XXX)
- Verify with your bank
- Ensure no spaces or extra characters

### Issue: "Tax information verification failed"
**Solution:**
- Ensure TFN/ABN is correct
- Verify name and address match ATO records
- Wait 24 hours and try again (ATO updates may take time)

### Issue: "Payment setup incomplete" banner persists
**Solution:**
- Complete all required fields
- Check for any error messages
- Ensure you've submitted (not just saved)
- Wait for email confirmation
